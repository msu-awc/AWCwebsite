Grace Hopper Celebration

Bringing research and career interests of women in computing to the forefront

Previous Next

The Grace Hopper Celebration (GHC) is the biggest conference for women technologists in the world, with thousands of attendees every year from all around the world. This major three-day event allows women in all stages of their careers to network and find job opportunities, mentors, and community with being a minor in our field. GHC hosts a career fair with over 800 companies, including Google, Apple, the National Security Agency (NSA), Boeing, and many more. The Technology Showcase with the GHC Expo presents latest products, research, and innovative breakthroughs. Dozens of presentations and workshops allow women to learn more about technologies like data science, security, and artifical intelligence, as well as career and social aspects to being a woman in STEM, like impostor syndrome, navigating the corporate ladder, and how to handle sexism in the field.


Montana State University strives to sponsor students to attend this life-changing event. We host info sessions about the conference and take applications for attendance during the spring semester of every school year. If you're selected, your GHC admission, boarding, and airfare will be paid for! For more information, contact Sharlyn at sharlyn.izurieta@montana.edu.
