Events

Catapalooza

ACM and AWC share a booth every year at Catapalooza to show students who we are and what we're all about! Stop by to get to know some of the CS students, sign up for our email list, and grab some candy and stickers.
Already involved? Ask your club officers how you can get involved!

CS Welcome Party

At the beginning of every fall semester, the CS department holds an awesome fundraiser party for all of the students, faculty, and staff of the CS department! Typically we have ice cream and fun carnival activities, like bean bag tossing, sidewalk chalk art, and more! Come by to connect with other students and your professors all while having fun!

Hour of Code

All around the world, Code.org hosts an event designed for all ages to learn coding with fun one-hour tutorials with all sorts of languages. Twice a year, the CS students at MSU invite kids K-12 onto campus to participate in learning how to code with Star Wars characters, Anna and Elsa from Frozen, and so many more! Students can contact AWC to learn how to volunteer in this fantastic event!

Cookies and Cram

AWC and ACM team up to take over parts of Norm and provide cookies, tea, hot cocoa while students join up to study for their final exams, work on their final projects, or whatever else is coming up!

General Events

If we don't have a guest speaker, panel, or special event, you'll find us hosting homework nights, game nights, decorating cookies, and so much more!
