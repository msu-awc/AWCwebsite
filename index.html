"There is no limit to what we, as women, can accomplish."

Here at the Assocation for Women in Computing, we strive to provide a supportive, positive community for members and to support gender diversity in computer science

Sign Up for our Mailing List

Guests Speakers

Speakers from across the country come visit AWC to present panels of interest, like empowerment workshops and what it's like working in the tech industry. We also have field trips to local companies, the American Computer and Robotics Museum.

Impactful Events

AWC prides itself on serving the women in our field and the people around us. We send many of our members to the Grace Hopper Conference, and provide interview practice for hard and soft skills.

Community

The best part of AWC is the sense of community in a male-dominated field. We help support one another through the academic and professional world. The students you meet here may become your friends for life.
